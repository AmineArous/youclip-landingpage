# What's New

<br>

### `Initial Release`
# **Version 1.0**

The first release of **YouClip**.

#### Core
- AI-powered summaries of YouTube videos, generated on-device with Apple Intelligence
- Share extension: send any YouTube video directly from the YouTube app to YouClip
- Automatic category detection adapts the summary to the type of video
- Multi-language summaries
- Full history, always one tap away

#### YouClip Pro
- Unlimited Apple Intelligence summaries
- Cloud summarization for longer videos
- Advanced summary modes: flashcards, quiz, key quotes, critical analysis, recipes, step-by-step
- Comment analysis — understand what viewers actually think
- Audio player: listen to summaries on the go
